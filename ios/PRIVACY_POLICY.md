# RoadRank Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## Introduction

RoadRank ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data

RoadRank collects location data to provide its core functionality:

- **When In Use**: We collect your location when the app is open to show your position on the map and help you discover nearby roads.
- **Background Location** (Optional): If you enable "Always Allow" location access, we can track your route while driving even when the app is in the background. This data is used solely to help you record driving routes.

### Road Data

When you create or rate a road:
- Road path coordinates
- Road name
- Your ratings (1-5 stars for each category)
- Optional comments

This data is stored on our servers and shared with other users of the app.

### Device Information

We may collect:
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Anonymous usage analytics

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

1. Display roads on the map
2. Enable you to draw and save new roads
3. Show community ratings and comments
4. Improve app performance and features
5. Provide location-based search

## Data Sharing

- **Public Road Data**: Roads and ratings you submit are visible to all app users
- **Personal Location**: Your real-time location is never shared with other users
- **Third Parties**: We do not sell your personal data to third parties

### Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **OpenStreetMap/Nominatim**: For location search (subject to OSM privacy policy)
- **OSRM (Open Source Routing Machine)**: For road path snapping
- **Supabase**: For data storage (subject to Supabase privacy policy)

## Data Retention

- Road and rating data is retained indefinitely as part of the community database
- You may request deletion of your contributed data by contacting us

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Secure server infrastructure
- Regular security updates

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your contributed data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of location tracking by denying permission

## Children's Privacy

RoadRank is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by other means.

## Location Permission Details

### "When In Use" Permission

When you grant "When In Use" location access, RoadRank can:
- Show your current position on the map
- Center the map on your location
- Calculate distance to roads near you

### "Always Allow" Permission

When you grant "Always Allow" location access, RoadRank can additionally:
- Track your route in the background while driving
- Suggest roads to rate based on routes you've driven
- Provide turn-by-turn position updates

You can change these permissions at any time in your device Settings.
